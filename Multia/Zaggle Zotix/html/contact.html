Why should I select a YES Bank Zaggle Cobranded Corporate Credit Card?

The YES Bank Zaggle co-branded Corporate Credit Card provides a 50-day interest-free period, allowing businesses to have more cash on hand to manage daily expenses. By providing these cards to employees and setting individual limits based on merchant codes, transaction frequency, and amounts, businesses can ensure that their employees stay within budget. These limits are automatically applied at the point of sale, preventing overspending. Additionally, the cards are integrated with Zaggle ZatiX, a SaaS-based spend analytics platform to analyse company spends. Corporates, who sign up for the cards, will also be able to access these insights for free.
