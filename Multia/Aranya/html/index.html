Explore and live the natural way with us.

Explore and live the ‘natural’ way with us. Aranya is a  privately owned agricultural estate, set amidst the untamed magnificence of the Phansad Reserve Forest, in the Raigad District, of Maharashtra, India.

It has well appointed stilt cottages, camouflaged by thick teak groves, are best suited for the discerning traveler, seeking an akin to home holiday experience. Our kitchens serve up delectable home cooked meals, inspired by local flavors and farm fresh produce. Plan your rejuvenating gateway at Aranya at Phansad.
