No limits to what my brush can create...

I’m always thrilled to work on projects in very diverse fields.

My illustrations have been featured throughout the design and fashion world, where I created a variety of fabric prints and brand identities. I collaborated with various restaurants in Milan, designing menu’s, painting recipes and advertising communication. As a passionate book reader, I illustrated several manuscripts including children books and cook books. I count many commisions for personalized invites, custom designs and unique gifts. Immagination is my asset and my focus is always on creating something elegant and memorable.
