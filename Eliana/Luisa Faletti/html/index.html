Meet The Artist

Growing up in Milan, Luisa cultivated her creative passion from a young age.
Always surrounded by visual beauty, she became quickly attracted by the world of art, design and fashion.
After achieving her Bachelor’s Degree in Milan at IED, with a degree in Illustration and Animation, she moved to New York City to specialise in Graphic Design at Parsons. She is now cultivating her dream and is a full-time illustrator and graphic designer - offering unique customised experiences to transform her clients visions into watercolour.
